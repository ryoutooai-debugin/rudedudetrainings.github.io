📗 What is a Stock?

A stock represents ownership in a company. When you buy a stock, you own a small piece of that company!

📈 Bull vs Bear Markets

A Bull Market means prices are going UP 📈
A Bear Market means prices are going DOWN 📉

🎯 Buy Low, Sell High

The goal of trading is to buy stocks when they're cheap and sell them when they're worth more!

Buy at $50 → Sell at $75 = $25 profit! 💰

📊 Reading Charts

Green bars mean the price went UP that day.
Red bars mean the price went DOWN that day.
