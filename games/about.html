📚 About & Safety

Learn trading the right way - safely and responsibly

🎯 Our Mission

RudeDude Trainings creates educational games and tools that teach financial literacy through hands-on experience. We believe learning about markets, trading, and investing should be:

  • Accessible - No prior knowledge required
  • Engaging - Learn through play, not lectures
  • Safe - Practice with fake money before risking real money
  • Practical - Skills you can use in real life

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 For Parents

What Your Kids Will Learn

  • Basic stock market concepts (stocks, bulls, bears)
  • Reading charts and understanding price movements
  • Risk management and portfolio diversification
  • Patience and emotional control in trading
  • The difference between investing and gambling
✅ Safety First
All our games use simulated money only. There is no way to spend real money or win real prizes. This creates a safe environment for learning without financial risk.

Age Recommendations

Ages 8-12 Trading Quest - Simple concepts, cartoon visuals, basic buy/sell mechanics

Ages 10-14 Color Match & Market Match - Pattern recognition, memory skills

Ages 13+ Portfolio Challenge - Real market data, advanced concepts, competition

Conversation Starters

  • "What did you learn about the stock market today?"
  • "Why do you think that stock went up/down?"
  • "How did you decide when to buy or sell?"
  • "What would you do differently next time?"

🛡️ Safety & Privacy

⚠️ Important Disclaimer
These games are for educational purposes only. Past performance in the game does not guarantee future results. Always consult with a qualified financial advisor before making real investment decisions.

Data Privacy

  • No personal information required - No names, emails, or addresses collected
  • Anonymous gameplay - Random user IDs only
  • Local storage - Game progress saved in your browser only
  • No tracking - No ads, no analytics, no cookies
  • Open source - All code is visible and auditable

Safe Trading Principles We Teach

  • ✅ Never risk money you can't afford to lose
  • ✅ Diversify - don't put all eggs in one basket
  • ✅ Have a plan (entry, exit, stop-loss)
  • ✅ Control emotions - fear and greed are dangerous
  • ✅ Learn before you earn - practice first
  • ✅ Time in the market beats timing the market

Warning Signs of Problematic Trading

Teach kids (and yourself) to watch for these red flags:

  • ❌ Trading with money needed for bills/rent
  • ❌ Chasing losses (trying to win back lost money)
  • ❌ Trading based on emotions or tips
  • ❌ Putting all money in one stock
  • ❌ Not having an exit plan

📖 Continued Learning

Recommended Resources

  • Books: "The Intelligent Investor" by Benjamin Graham, "A Random Walk Down Wall Street" by Burton Malkiel
  • Websites: Investopedia.com, SEC.gov (investor education)
  • Videos: Khan Academy (Finance & Capital Markets)
  • Podcasts: "The Investors Podcast," "Motley Fool Money"

Next Steps

  1. Master the games - practice until concepts are clear
  2. Paper trade - use fake money on real platforms (many brokers offer this)
  3. Start small - when ready, invest small amounts you can afford to lose
  4. Keep learning - markets evolve, so should your knowledge
  5. Think long-term - wealth is built over years, not days
🎓 Remember: The goal isn't to get rich quick. It's to build knowledge and habits that lead to financial security over a lifetime.
